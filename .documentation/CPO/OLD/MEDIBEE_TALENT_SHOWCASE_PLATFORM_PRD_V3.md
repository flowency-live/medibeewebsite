
# MEDIBEE TALENT SHOWCASE PLATFORM
## Product Requirements Document (PRD) — v3

---

# 1. Platform Definition

Medibee is a **talent visibility and trust platform for Healthcare Assistants (HCAs).**

It enables:
- verified professional identity
- exposure to care providers
- controlled introductions
- early‑career talent visibility
- workforce trust signalling

Medibee is **NOT**:
- a job board
- a shift booking system
- a payroll provider
- a staffing agency operations system
- a full workforce management SaaS
- a regulated care service

### Category

**Professional Identity + Verified Trust + Workforce Discovery (verticalised for HCAs)**

Mental model:
- LinkedIn → identity
- DocLibrary → credential trust
- Recruitment agency → human introductions
- University careers platform → early talent pipeline

# 2. Core Problem Space

## Care Providers
- Cannot easily see available talent
- Must engage multiple agencies
- Re‑verify credentials repeatedly
- Poor cultural / environment fit
- Reactive staffing behaviour

## Healthcare Assistants
- Passive job seeking model
- CVs hidden in email inboxes
- No professional branding
- Repeated onboarding friction
- No visibility into employer environments

## Universities
- Cannot demonstrate graduate employment outcomes
- No structured showcase for placement experience
- Limited employer exposure channels

# 3. Product Pillars

## 3.1 Professional Digital Identity Layer

Each HCA has a **public professional showcase page.**

### Profile Components
- professional summary
- experience timeline
- care setting expertise
- availability signalling
- testimonials
- employer feedback
- profile video introduction
- certifications display
- university verification badge
- micro‑credentials

### Verifiable Credential Wallet
- DBS status
- right to work
- immunisations
- training certificates
- diplomas
- mandatory competencies

### Capabilities
- QR verification link
- credential expiry alerts
- compliance readiness indicators
- shareable trust profile

---

## 3.2 Exposure & Discovery Engine

Platform enables:
- provider browsing of verified talent
- filtering by location / experience / setting
- shortlisting
- introduction requests
- availability visibility

### Candidate Exposure Logic
- relevance ranking
- proximity weighting
- experience matching
- soft skill signals (future AI scoring)
- platform engagement signals

Medibee facilitates introductions rather than enabling direct marketplace transactions.

## 3.4 University & Early Talent Pipeline

University integration features:

---

## 3.5 Light Workforce Operational Signals

Optional employer tools:

Purpose:
- workforce trust signalling
- operational confidence indicators

---

# 4. Critical Guardrails (Where Medibee Must NOT Go)

Medibee must NOT:
- dispatch shifts directly
- manage payroll
- own employment contracts
- provide rostering systems
- compete with agency operational tooling
- provide clinical governance systems
- provide care delivery services

Medibee wins by being:
**The identity + trust + discovery layer sitting above staffing execution.**
